Company overview

Shah transport was begun by my father in 1980. The point of my father is to give Jobs to people groups and he instructed me that consistently be the sort of individual who offers responsibilities to individuals. We have been in this business for a very long time we are master around here.

At Shah Transport, we know time is of the essence. We have used our legacy Truckload service in the southern regions to shape what our company is today. In addition to Truckload services, we have expanded our reach on the asset-based side to include expedited services and volume Less-Than-Truckload as well as a compliment of non-asset based solutions to service our customer's needs throughout south India.

The commitment we have to safety and security is unmatched. We pride ourselves on posting industry-leading Compliance, Safety, Accountability (CSA) scores, as well as an industry-low claims ratio to give you and your customers that piece of mind when your load is in our hands. Our primary obligation to our customers and our community starts with the hiring and training of our team members and our pledge to put the safest drivers and equipment on the road.

From our experienced drivers to our knowledgeable office staff, Shah Transport has the desire and commitment to work hand-in-hand to develop a transportation solution that is truly customized to your needs.
